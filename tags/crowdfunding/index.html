Crowdfunding

Beauty Premium versus Beauty Discount in Crowdfunding Platforms

(Joint work with Jooyoung Park and Ying-yi Hong) Examine the effect of physical attractiveness of recipients on donations decisions in a prosocial microlending market by combining both the archival data analysis and controlled experiments

Complements Rather Than Substitutes: An Empirical Examination of Cryptocurrency and Online Peer-to-Peer Lending Markets

(Joint work with Sunghun Chung) Examine the relationship between cryptocurrency markets and P2P lending markets

Disclosure of Project Risk in Crowdfunding

(Joint work with Jooyoung Park, Yang Pan, Kunpeng Zhang, and Michael Zhang) Examine the effect of the disclosure of project risk on crowdfunders with particular focus on project type

Effect of Crowdfunding Success on Subsequent Financing Outcomes of Start-ups

(Joint work with Sunghan Ryu and Jungpil Hahn) Examine the effect of early funding (crowdfunding vs. angel investing) on VC funding

When and How Much to Pledge: The Role of Reward versus Altruistic Motivations in Predicting Crowdfunding Pledges

(Joint work with Sunghan Ryu and Jooyoung Park) Examine the effect of contributor motivations on crowdfunding pledges
